About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Information Systems and Applications at National Tsing Hua University, working with Prof. Hwann-Tzong Chen (at VLLab) and Prof. Min Sun (at VSLab). My research interests encompass wide application of computer vision, such as 360 panorama, scene modeling and understanding, object detection. Currently, I'm working on multiviews 3D reconstruction.
